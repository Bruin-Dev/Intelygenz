    Service Affecting Monitor Event Logging

    Description

    The Service Affecting Monitor service has three responsibilities:

    • Monitor VeloCloud edges periodically. The monitoring process makes sure to create tickets or re-open Service Affecting tasks if these edges are experiencing issues that impact the quality of their service, and it also takes care of auto-resolving any open ticket tasks if the edge stabilizes.

      Monitored Service Affecting troubles are:

      • Latency
      • Packet Loss
      • Jitter
      • Bandwidth Over-Utilization
      • Circuit Instability

    • Deliver Reoccurring Trouble Reports weekly. These reports include a relation of edges' links and the tickets where at least one of the Service Affecting troubles has been reported at least a few times in a specific period of time.

    • Deliver Daily Bandwidth Reports. These reports include a relation of edges' links that have experienced Bandwidth Over-Utilization troubles, and the tickets where those troubles were documented.
